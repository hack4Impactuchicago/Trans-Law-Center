Petition form for legal name and/or gender change
Transgender Law Center (TLC) and Hack4Impact-UChicago have developed a tool to help you with the process of filling out court forms to petition for a legal name and/or gender change in California. Use this tool to access printable and fillable blank forms, step-by-step instructions for filling out the forms, and sample filled-out forms. Anyone currently living in California can use these forms and directions.
For more information about the process of obtaining a name and/or gender change and updating your California and federal identification, please refer to TLC’s guide “ID Please!” available at https://transgenderlawcenter.org/resources/id/id-please.
The information on this site should not be considered legal advice. This site provides you with legal information but does not seek to apply that information to your specific situation.
If you have a question about your specific legal situation, feel free to contact Transgender Law Center’s Legal Information Helpline at www.transgenderlawcenter.org/legalinfo and we’ll do our best to answer your question or point you to resources that may be helpful.
